Data Mining

It is known for data exploration, where its techniques enable the analysis of small details in small or large volumes of data, in order to discover patterns, profiles or other relationships that are of interest.




Big Data

Big Data is the term used in general for what is called “Big Data”, it seeks the relationships of data in immense sets and in a general overview. This technology is capable of capturing, managing and processing this data at high speed, providing reliable data that is converted into quality information, consequently increasing the productivity of companies.
