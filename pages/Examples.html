Data Mining

In general, the main uses of data mining are verified in:
  • Digital marketing operations management.
  • Identification of customer defection patterns.
  • Prediction of customer movements by subscription.




Big data

Big data techniques in logistics make it possible to optimize processes, particularly those that make up the supply chain. Among which it is worth highlighting: the supply, slotting and picking processes, the planning and monitoring of routes and last mile routes, schedules and delivery times, information on weather conditions, fuel savings, the use of big data in e -commerce, user behavior on the network, stock control.
